About Us

We are ZBS Group

ZBS Groups is a diversified investment holding company with an interest in various sectors of the Nigerian economy. We commenced business operations in 2021. Our objective is to carry out foreign exchange-related services, financial ancillary services, and other support services. We are passionate about providing our clients with the best possible trading experience in the forex market. We believe that our dedication to transparency, integrity, and client satisfaction sets us apart from other trading companies. Our company was founded by a team of experienced traders who saw a need for a more client-focused and transparent approach to forex trading.
